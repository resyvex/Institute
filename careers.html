Career Opportunities at Resyvex Institute

Be part of our mission to transform lives through quality education

Resyvex Institute is growing rapidly and we're looking for passionate professionals to join our team. We offer competitive salaries, flexible working arrangements, professional development opportunities, and a collaborative work environment. If you're passionate about education and technology, we'd love to hear from you!

Open Positions

Trainer / Instructor Roles

Electronics & PCB Design Trainer

We're looking for experienced professionals to teach electronics, PCB design, and embedded systems courses.

Requirements:
• 3+ years of industry experience in electronics or PCB design
• Strong knowledge of analog, digital, and embedded systems
• Experience with design tools (Altium, KiCad, or similar)
• Excellent communication and teaching skills
• Bachelor's degree in Electronics or equivalent

Location: On-site & Remote | Full-time / Part-time

Embedded Systems & Microcontroller Trainer

Deliver engaging training on microcontrollers, RTOS, and IoT systems to aspiring engineers.

Requirements:
• 2+ years of hands-on embedded systems experience
• Proficiency in C/C++ and embedded C
• Knowledge of AVR, ARM, or PIC microcontrollers
• Experience with RTOS (FreeRTOS or similar)
• Passion for mentoring and teaching

Location: On-site & Remote | Full-time / Part-time

FPGA & Hardware Design Trainer

Lead training in FPGA design, Verilog, VHDL, and hardware description languages.

Requirements:
• 3+ years of FPGA design experience
• Expert in Verilog/VHDL
• Knowledge of Xilinx or Altera tools
• Experience with high-speed digital design
• Strong problem-solving and teaching abilities

Location: On-site | Full-time

Academic Coordinator Roles

Academic Coordinator - Electronics Department

Manage curriculum development, student progress, and coordinate with trainers for program excellence.

Responsibilities:
• Develop and update course curricula
• Monitor student progress and performance
• Coordinate between trainers and students
• Manage course schedules and materials
• Conduct academic audits and improvements
• Maintain quality standards

Location: On-site | Full-time

Program Manager - Technical Training

Oversee all aspects of technical training programs, from planning to execution and assessment.

Responsibilities:
• Design and implement training programs
• Manage trainer performance and development
• Assess program effectiveness
• Liaise with industry partners
• Ensure compliance with standards
• Report metrics and analytics

Location: On-site | Full-time

Student Support Executive

Provide 24/7 support to students, answer queries, and ensure excellent learning experience.

Responsibilities:
• Handle student queries via phone, email, chat
• Resolve technical and academic issues
• Maintain student records
• Schedule mentor sessions
• Follow up on student progress
• Gather feedback for improvements

Location: On-site & Remote | Full-time / Part-time

Placement & Career Counselor

Guide students through their career journey and help them secure suitable job opportunities.

Responsibilities:
• Conduct career counseling sessions
• Resume review and guidance
• Interview preparation coaching
• Job matching and placements
• Maintain recruiter relationships
• Track placement metrics

Location: On-site | Full-time

Content Developer & Curriculum Designer

Create engaging course materials, videos, and learning resources for our programs.

Responsibilities:
• Develop course materials and content

• Design practical assignments and projects
• Review and update existing content
• Coordinate with trainers for input
• Ensure content quality and relevance
