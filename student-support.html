Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access student support services?

You can access support through our online portal, email, phone, live chat, or visit our centers in person.

What is the response time for support queries?

We aim to respond to all queries within 24 hours. Urgent issues may be addressed within 1-2 hours.

Are support services included in the course fee?

Yes, all support services are included in your course fee. No additional charges for support services.

Can I get help with assignments?

Yes, we provide guidance and support for assignments, projects, and practical work throughout the course.

Is mentorship available for all students?

Yes, all students have access to our mentorship program. You can request a mentor assigned based on your needs.
