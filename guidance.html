Career Guidance & Counseling

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the guidance session really free?

Yes! Our free guidance sessions are completely free of cost. We provide personalized career counseling without any charges.

How long is each session?

Each guidance session typically lasts between 45 minutes to 1 hour, depending on the complexity of your situation.

Can I schedule multiple sessions?

Yes, you can schedule follow-up sessions if needed. We provide continuous guidance throughout your learning journey.

What should I prepare for the session?

Come with an open mind and your career goals. Bring any documents related to your academic background and work experience if available.

Is the session confidential?

Absolutely! All information shared during the session is completely confidential and used only for your benefit.
